Information on Covid-19

Coronavirus or Covid-19, is an asymptomatic novel virus that has caused major disruptions on a global scale. In order for society to return back to normal, it is important for us to be able to recognize symptoms of the virus and do our part in reducing the spread of the virus.

  1. Symptoms of Coronavirus

    • Fever or chills
    • Shortness of breath or difficulty in breathing
    • Dry cough and sore throat

  2. Ways to prevend the spread of Coronavirus

    • Wear a mask when coming in close contact with others and when entering public places.
    • Wash your hands often with soap and water or with hand sanitizer.
    • Exercise social distancing; stay at home when you feel sick and maintain a 6 foot distance between yourself and people who don't live in your household.

  3. Relevant Coronavirus Statistics (September 29, 2020)

    • There are 124k positive recorded Covid-19 cases in Maryland.
    • There have been 3938 confirmed deaths due to Covid-19 in Maryland.
    • The University of Maryland has 776 total positive Covid-19 cases and a 1.1% positivity rate.
